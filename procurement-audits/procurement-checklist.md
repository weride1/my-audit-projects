# Procurement Checklist (Audit Focus)

## Key Controls to Test

- [ ] Requisition approval by authorized person
- [ ] Purchase order matches requisition
- [ ] Goods receipt verified before payment
- [ ] Invoice three-way match (PO, GRN, Invoice)
- [ ] Vendor master data reviewed periodically
- [ ] Duplicate payments detected via system rules

## Risk Areas
- Split purchases to avoid approval thresholds
- Unauthorized vendors added without due diligence
- Late or missing approvals leading to delays
